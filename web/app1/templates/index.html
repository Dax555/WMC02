About the Game

Quidditch is a popular wizarding sport played at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. It involves two teams of seven players who fly on broomsticks and compete to score points by throwing a ball called the Quaffle through the opponent's hoops while also defending against flying Bludgers and pursuing the elusive Golden Snitch for a significant point boost.
